Holert Consulting

Founded by brothers, Patrick and Michael Holert, we are a boutique consulting firm providing high-quality, cost-effective services through a lens of practical real-world experience in managing companies.

Our Services

Litigation Support

Our litigation support services include expert witness testimony, financial analysis, forensic accounting and estimating damages. We are currently serving as an expert witness in multiple cases involving cryptocurrency mining.

Business Consulting

Business consulting services include developing company strategy, establishing accounting systems, reviewing and negotiating agreements, preparing policies and procedures, creating investment memorandum and providing valuations.

Management and Director Roles

We can serve in short-term or long-term management roles, including as Chief Executive Officer, Chief Operating Officer, Chief Financial Officer and Chief Restructuring Officer. Additionally, we serve as Independent Directors.

Restructuring and Turnaround

For companies needing restructuring or turnaround services, we can prepare business plans and forecasts, restructure debt, negotiate with stakeholders, reduce costs and create efficiencies.

Industry Experience

Industries that we have experience in include: cryptocurrency mining, financial services, sports & entertainment, manufacturing, E-commerce and accounting.
